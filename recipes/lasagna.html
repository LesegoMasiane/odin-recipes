DELICIOUS LASAGNA


Description


Melted Cheese brings together noodles and amazing ingredients

Cooking time is as follows: 2 hours 30 minutes, worth the wait.


Ingredients
  • Noodle bases x 27
  • Mince meat / Vegetarian substitute 2 kg
  • Love of cooking

Steps
  1. Boil the noodle base for 10 minutes until al dente
  2. Prepare the mince on a separate pan or buy from the store
  3. Throw it in the pre-heated oven until the top is golden brown
